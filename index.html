About Me

Innocent Maduhu holds two master’s degrees from Georgia State University—MS in Actuarial Science (Dec 2024) and MS in Quantitative Risk Analysis and Management (May 2025)—and an MSc in Actuarial Science with distinction from BSA Crescent Institute of Science & Technology (Dec 2018). He began his journey with a Bachelor’s in Actuarial Science from the University of Dar es Salaam (Nov 2015), where he served as a Tutorial Assistant preparing assignments, grading exams, guiding projects, and coordinating actuarial career events.

Driven by his passion for a career as an actuary, Innocent pursued dual master’s studies at GSU in 2023, aiming for the ASA designation by mid-2026. His internship at Reinsurance Group of America in Chesterfield, Missouri, saw him collaborate across underwriting and pricing teams, leveraging Advanced Excel, VBA, and Power BI to analyze, automate, and present data effectively.
